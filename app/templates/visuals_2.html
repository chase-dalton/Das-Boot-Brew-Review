How Can We Predict Beer Tasting Good?



By utilizing our innovative Machine Learning algorithm, Das Boot can help your brewery save time and money, make high-quality beer, and free up company resources for creativity and innovation.






What goes features are important when reviewing if a beer will be good or not? Our algerithm measures several different features focusing on ABV, or alcohol by volume, appearance, aroma, and color. We compare the overall score, palate, and review count against taste to dermine what makes a good beer.



We used a supervised machine learning module to test what features come into play when determining if a beer is good or not.

Based on our findings, the ABV doesn't play any part on whether or not a beer tastes good.




The next feature we tested out was whether or not appearance had any affect on taste. While it may be shocking at first to see that there is a positive correlation between the appearance and taste of the beer, the reasoning makes sense. The type of glass that the beer is served in, the head and the head retention, and the lacing after taking a nice long sip all play an important factor when it comes to the appearance of the beer.




How many people want to drink a beer that smells like the men's locker room? Not very many with the aroma being one of the most important features when it comes to determining a good beer. Smell is such an important part of determining if we like something, and that doesn't stop for beer.




The color has no correlation when it comes to how well a beeer tastes.
